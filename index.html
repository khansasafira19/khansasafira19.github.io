Phenomena Recording System

Aplikasi perekaman data fenomena (ekonomi). Fiturnya berupa kategorisasi (manual) fenomena dalam PDRB lapus dan pengeluaran, alat bantu Gooogle CSE, manajemen pengguna, tabulasi fenomena, monitoring fenomena. Aplikasi ini adalah objek paper penelitian saya (terlampir di GitHub-nya), dipakai di tempat kerja saya (versi unmodified-nya) dan sedang dikembangkan versi upgrade nya.

  • Basis: Yii2 PHP Framework, MySQL Database, AdminLTE3

Link: https://github.com/khansasafira19/sk-phenomena-recording-portal.

Employees' Jobs Management System

Aplikasi manajemen penugasan kerja pegawai di kantor. Berisi manajemen laporan pekerjaan harian, presensi harian, manajemen penugasan, manajemen tim kerja, manajemen pengguna dan executive summary.

  • Basis: Yii2 PHP Framework, MySQL Database, AdminLTE3

Link: https://github.com/khansasafira19/sk-employee-job-manager.

Eduprime Course

Aplikasi untuk Lembaga Les/Sekolah. Isinya manajemen materi PDF dan YouTube Videos, manajemen siswa/admin, CAT Simulation (latihan soal dengan timer dan pembahasan), manajemen soal (dapat tambah paket soal, soal bergambar dsb).

  • Basis: Yii2 PHP Framework, MySQL, Bootstrapmade (NiceAdmin dan Bootslander)

Link: https://github.com/khansasafira19/sk-eduprime-course.

Laman Data Storytelling

Laman berisi storytelling berdasarkan data nelayan dan kemiskinan di Provinsi Bengkulu. Laman ini pernah diikutkan lomba data storytelling namun belum beruntung mendapatkan predikat juara.

  • Basis: HTML, JavaScript, Data AOS

Link: https://github.com/khansasafira19/sk-cool-storytelling.

Virtual Playbutton

Laman berisi virtual button yang jika diklik menampilkan confetti dan memutar audio. Dapat digunakan untuk event yang membutuhkan kick-off mark.

  • Basis: HTML, JavaScript

Link: https://github.com/khansasafira19/sk-virtual-playbutton.

Interactive Infographics

Laman berisi infografis interaktif berupa data kemiskinan, pengangguran dan penduduk tahun 2015. Dibangun tahun 2016, alhamdulillah mendapatkan juara 3 kategori Infografis Interaktif pada perlombaan desain infografis yang diselenggarakan oleh BPS RI.

  • HTML, JavaScript dan template yang maaf saya lupa diambil dimana.

Link: https://github.com/khansasafira19/sk-interactive-infographics.

EDUCATION AND OCCUPATION

I graduated from Institute of Statistics (Politeknik Statistika STIS) in 2013, majored in Computation Statistics.

I'm currently studying for a master's degree in Management. Wish me all the best!

I'm currently working as an expert computer specialist for Statistics of Bengkulu Province. I used to develop apps for business and handle data processing in my unit. But now, I've been assigned to focus more on developing IT innovations, apps, and information systems.

I conducted an unofficial online school that taught English generally for free, for Indonesians aged 15 – 64 years. Subjects included Grammar, Writing, Speaking and Listening. The school had accepted at least 1000 students and generated at least 300 alumni. Now, an alumnus works together with me to run another class, it is called PINTAR (Program Belajar Bahasa Inggris Terpadu). You can join by texting this number: Ms. Ervi Wulandari

I provided translation service from Indonesian to English vice versa for clients in Indonesia on academic research papers, thesis abstracts and books.
